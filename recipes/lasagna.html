Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is perfect for any family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer the pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes.
