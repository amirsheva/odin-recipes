Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is perfect for any family dinner. Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese make this dish a favorite!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (Ricotta, Mozzarella, Parmesan)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the ground beef with chopped onions and garlic until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes to create the meat sauce.
  4. In a baking dish, layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese. Repeat the layers.
  5. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
