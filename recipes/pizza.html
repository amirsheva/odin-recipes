Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

This classic pizza recipe is perfect for pizza lovers. A simple dough with a rich tomato sauce, fresh toppings, and melted cheese makes it irresistible.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Bell peppers
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired size.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add toppings like cheese, pepperoni, and bell peppers.
  5. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot!
