Salad Recipe

Home

Description

This refreshing salad recipe is loaded with fresh vegetables, a tangy dressing, and a sprinkle of herbs for a healthy and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion as desired.
  3. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss well to mix the dressing evenly and serve immediately.
